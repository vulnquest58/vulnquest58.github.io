### Red Team Fundamentals Notes

* **Red Team Engagements**: Adversary emulation where the red team emulates realistic threat profiles targeting people, processes, and technology.
* **Rules of Engagement (RoE)**: A defining document outlining the boundaries of the test, including targets, scope, hours of testing, communication plans, and emergency contacts.
* **TTPs**: Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures.
* **C2 Setup**: Deploying redirection networks and teamservers to handle command and control agents.
