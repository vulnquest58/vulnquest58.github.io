### Red Team Engagement lifecycle

1. **Information Gathering**: Active and passive reconnaissance on the target.
2. **Threat Emulation**: Emulating specific advanced persistent threat (APT) playbooks.
3. **Exploitation & Pivoting**: Initial access followed by credential theft and network hopping.
4. **Post-Engagement**: Cleanup of payloads, restoration of configurations, and a debriefing report detailing paths, detections, and defense gaps.
